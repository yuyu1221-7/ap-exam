Chemistry

Section I has 60 multiple-choice questions and lasts 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Each of the questions or incomplete statements is followed by 4 suggested answers or completions. Select the one that is best in each case.

For all questions, assume that the temperature is 298 K, the pressure is 1.0 atm, and solutions are aqueous unless otherwise specified.

A calculator is allowed in this section. You may use a handheld four-function, scientific, or graphing calculator, or the calculator available in this application.
